About Us

We stock and sell only high quality goods that will give the angler ( YOU ) an advantage over other anglers. We want to let you enjoy fishing with high technology products that feel good and are easy to use.

All our products come from reputable sources. We do not sell fake, counterfeit or imitating products. We insist only on the best quality items available in the world today, products that have been tried and proven. We believe in helping anglers make the best of their time on the water and justify the high costs involved in going fishing these days, by increasing their catch results. We are not interested in going fishing with the bare essentials and hope to get lucky. Lucky anglers make their own luck, by investing in quality gear and armed with the best knowledge available.

We provide goods at a minimal charge. All other intangible services are free of charge. Spooling of line, advice on how to use our products, referrals, references, pre-rigging of products purchased from us, information on fish species, fishing tactics, information regarding the latest tackle, how to match your tackle correctly etc. We are happy to share our immense knowledge free of charge with our customers.

Shipping

We take great care packaging every item to ensure safe shipment to you. Please allow 1-2 working days for processing orders. Most items will ship next working day of receiving a cleared payment. Please take into consideration weekends and holidays when estimating shipping time. All orders will ship out within 2-3 working days.

All import taxes and duties payable is the responsibility of the buyer alone.

FREE WORLDWIDE SHIPPING BY NORMAL MAIL

Orders less than $100 will be shipped by normal airmail. Estimated shipping time for this service is about 3-6 weeks with no tracking number. Normal airmail shipping time is especially slow for countries such as Latvia, Lithuania, Reunion Island, Israel, UAE, Turkey, All middle east countries, All Africa countries, Russia, Brazil and all of South America. For these countries, shipping time is estimated at about 6-8 weeks.

Customers who need their orders urgently, please purchase over the minimum values for Fedex shipping. Otherwise, please contact us to pay for Fedex service.

FREE WORLDWIDE SHIPPING BY FEDEX EXPRESS MAIL

Orders with minimum value $100 will be shipped by Fedex Express to most parts of the world.

Orders with minimum value $200 will be shipped by Fedex for countries such as Latvia, Lithuania, Reunion Island, Israel, UAE, Turkey, All middle east countries, All Africa countries, Brazil and all of South America.

Customers with orders shipped by Fedex will receive your tracking information via email. Shipping time is usually about 1 week excluding customs delays and checks. You can track your parcel on www.fedex.com

Customers, who are unsure which group your country falls under, may feel free to contact us.

MAILING TO AUSTRALIA

Orders with minimum value of $100 will be shipped via FedEx Express to most major cities. (e.g Sydney, Melbourne)

Cities that are more remote will be shipped via registered airmail. Shipping time for this service is 1-2 weeks with full detailed tracking status.

Customers with any doubts may feel free to contact us to check which service their order will be shipped with. (Please include your full address in your inquiry for faster and more accurate advice)

MAILING TO RUSSIA

Due to customs regulations, there will be no express shipping services to Russia.

We are also unable to ship rods into Russia due to this regulation.

All orders will be shipped via registered airmail. Shipping time is around 6 - 8 weeks.
